About the founders

Cravengers was founded by four second-year Iskolars ng Bayan from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, inspired by a shared commitment to use their education in service of the nation.

Rooted in the guiding words of their alma mater, “Gagamitin ang karunungan, mula sa’yo, para sa bayan”, they envisioned Cravengers as more than just a food delivery platform. It is their way of turning knowledge into action: a tool to empower local food vendors, promote sustainability, and contribute to community-building through technology.

Cravengers is their answer to the call of service — built by scholars, for the people, one meaningful meal at a time.
